Energy

Italy New Energy is a wholesaler in the Italian electric power market. We are involved in electric power trading for companies and operate on the Italian Power Exchange.

Gas

We are also active in the gas market. Italy New Energy is a natural gas wholesaler in the Italian market. As it is authorized to sell to end users.

Services

Italy New Energy offers companies not only electric power and gas. Our close relationship with the most important European electric power and gas producers and traders, allows us to offer competitive prices.

We can identify the best electricity and gas supply proposals, by comparing the various offers and evaluating potential savings. We can keep our clients updated on changes in laws and regulations on a timely basis, analyze the feasibility of new production plants and the revamping of existing facilities, and optimize plant operations in terms of cost efficiency and environmental compliance. What's more, we promote energy saving initiatives, manage the need for and availability and trading of green certificates (Italy New Energy's and other producers'), and coordinate and manage “Emission Trading”, the trading of CO 2 emissions credits.
