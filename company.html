Italy New Energy Profile

Italy New Energy is an Italian company founded in 2001.Italy New Energy is one of the leading suppliers of integrated products and services-from the supply of electric power and natural gas to the production of hydroelectric and thermoelectric energy and the development of projects and businesses in the renewable energy sectors.

Italy New Energy can provide services and consulting in the following fields: energy-saving project management and promotion, CO 2 emission shares and rights management, white and green certificate trading and power requirement management, and electricity and gas supply proposal evaluation and rating.
